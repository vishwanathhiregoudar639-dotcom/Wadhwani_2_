The Problem

Blind Curves on Ghat Roads Are Deadly

Ghat roads wind through mountainous terrain with sharp, blind curves where drivers cannot see oncoming traffic. Poor visibility, combined with narrow roads and steep gradients, leads to frequent head-on collisions, especially during fog, rain, and night conditions.

Our Solution

Intelligent Warning System for Ghat Road Safety

GhatGuard deploys smart sensors at blind curves to detect approaching vehicles and warn drivers on the opposite side in real-time.

Sensor-Based Vehicle Detection

Advanced IoT sensors detect approaching vehicles from a safe distance, accurately identifying vehicle type, speed, and direction even in poor visibility conditions.

Real-Time Audio & Visual Alerts

Bright LED warning displays and audio sirens activate instantly when a vehicle is detected, alerting drivers on the opposite side of the blind curve to slow down.

Easy Installation & Low Maintenance

Modular design allows quick deployment on any curve. Solar-powered options eliminate wiring needs. Built with weather-resistant materials for years of trouble-free operation.

Reliable in All Weather

Engineered to perform flawlessly in rain, fog, extreme heat, and cold. IP67-rated enclosures protect electronics while sensors operate through any weather condition.
